Iterative Design

Intro

Our group made an mobile phone app interface for the startup NFTScoring , which is an NFT buying and selling platform. We think that this interface will affect anybody who is interested in owning NFTs or people trying to learn more about NFTs in general. These people will be effected because our app is designed to be a user-friendly, and welcoming platform designed for people with little to no experience with NFTs to those that know them in and out.

Sketches and First Wireframe

Figma Prototypes

Our prototype that we presented during studio can be found here. Overall, feedback was mostly positive with the main suggestions being differentiate the buy and sell pages, add an "add favorites" function, give users more flexibility to edit their profile, and our overall dark mode was hard to read. With this feedback, we redesigned the interface with more attractive colors to drown out the black background. On top of this we added an option to favorite a particular NFT, and gave users a better experience when viewing their own profile. The finished result can be found here.

User Testing

The task we gave during our user testing was as follows:

1. Buy an NFT from the Space Riders collection
2. View your own NFT collection
3. Add the Space Riders NFT collection to your favorites
